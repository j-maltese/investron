# Investron v0.1.16 — Paper Trading with Simple Stock AI

**Date:** 2026-03-03

## Overview

Release 0.1.16 includes 6 new features, 1 bug fix.

---

## New Features

- Automated paper trading infrastructure via Alpaca Markets API
- Simple Stock AI strategy ($500 paper) — two-tier signal with screener filter + GPT-4o confirmation
- Trading engine background loop (60s cycles during market hours)
- RAG-enhanced trade signals — daily auto-indexes SEC filings for top screener candidates, injects filing excerpts into GPT-4o prompts
- Trading page with Overview, Positions, Orders, and Activity tabs
- Activity Feed with filter pills, expandable JSONB detail rows, date range picker, and load-more pagination
