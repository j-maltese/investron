**Date:** 2026-03-03

## New Features

- The Wheel options strategy — state machine: sell puts, get assigned, sell calls, called away, repeat
- Option selection with delta/yield/DTE scoring and moneyness fallback when greeks unavailable
- Defensive suite: hard stops (25%), rolling puts, adjusted cost basis tracking, capital efficiency exits (60 day), PDT protection
- Comprehensive observability: decision logs, execution logs, and blocked/restriction logs with full JSONB details
- Wheel Strategy Guide tab in Documentation page (7 sections)
